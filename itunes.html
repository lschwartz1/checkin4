Buying Songs on Itunes

Buying songs on Itunes is a fairly simple process. If you have already input your credit/debit card information, there are just a few simple steps left to purchasing a song on Itunes. The following steps should help you as you make your first few song purchases.

How to Buy Songs

  1. Open up the Itunes app on your desktop
  2. Click on the Itunes store button at the top of the screen
  3. After entering the Itunes store, go to the search bar at the upper right part of the screen
  4. Type in the name of the artist or song you are looking for and press Enter
  5. Look through the list the appears for the song you are looking for
  6. After finding the song you want, click the price button to the right of it
  7. Another screen should open asking you if you are sure you want to buy this song. If you are sure, press buy
  8. The song should start to download into your Itunes library
  9. Find the song in your library and listen to your heart's desire!
